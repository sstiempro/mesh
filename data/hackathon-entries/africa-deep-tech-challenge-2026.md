### Title
Mesh: Autonomous, Open-Source Crypto-Intelligence and Security Engine

### One-liner
Mesh is a running, autonomous system providing keyless crypto-intelligence and security monitoring, leveraging machine learning and AI for real-time insights and early warnings across DeFi and crypto markets.

### Problem
The cryptocurrency and DeFi spaces are plagued by volatility, exploits, and information overload, making it challenging for participants to stay informed and secure. Current solutions often rely on proprietary data, require extensive manual intervention, or lack the autonomy to adapt to emerging threats and opportunities.

### What we built
Mesh is an open-source, autonomous crypto-intelligence and security engine that has been shipping and running since its inception. Key components include:
- **~98 live keyless monitors** operating on a 24/7 cron schedule, covering stablecoin depegging, protocol TVL drain exploits, DeFi hack ledgers, cross-venue funding arbitrage, yield radar, token unlock calendars, Snapshot governance watches, Deribit DVOL vol-regime, Polymarket prediction market odds, whale flow, narrative rotation, and macro risk-on/off regime clocks.
- **Monitor-forge.mjs**: An autonomous capability generation engine that discovers new opportunity categories, uses a Large Language Model (LLM) to write new monitors, sandbox-verifies them, and ships them live without human intervention if they pass verification.
- **Smart-contract audit pipeline** utilizing Slither, Aderyn, and Foundry for public goods security, with diff-aware re-auditing of only changed code.
- **Multi-chain wallet** supporting EVM, Solana, Bitcoin, Cosmos, Sui, Aptos, and Near addresses, all derived from a single seed.
- **Open-core public signal feed** (RSS/JSON) with a free tier for the commons and a pro tier for sustainability.
- Mesh relies on **100% keyless and free public data sources** (DefiLlama, Hyperliquid, CoinGecko, Snapshot, Deribit, public RPCs), ensuring no proprietary data moats and full reproducibility.
