### Title
Retroactive Funding for Mesh: Autonomous, Open-Source Crypto-Intelligence and Security Engine

### One-liner
Mesh is a fully operational, keyless, and open-source crypto-intelligence and security engine providing public goods, including exploit early-warning systems and smart contract audit pipelines, seeking retroactive funding to sustain and expand its impact.

### Problem
The cryptocurrency and DeFi spaces are plagued by exploits, hacks, and security breaches, resulting in significant financial losses for users. The lack of accessible, reliable, and autonomous security tools exacerbates this issue, leaving users vulnerable to attacks. Moreover, the absence of open-source and reproducible solutions hinders the development of a robust and secure ecosystem.

### What we built
Mesh has shipped and is running with the following verifiable, public artifacts:
- 79 live keyless monitors on a 24/7 cron, covering stablecoin depeg, protocol TVL-drain exploit early-warning, DeFi hack ledger, cross-venue funding arbitrage, yield radar, token-unlock calendar, Snapshot governance watch, Deribit DVOL vol-regime, Polymarket prediction-market odds, whale flow, narrative rotation, and macro risk-on/off regime clock.
- A smart-contract audit pipeline utilizing Slither, Aderyn, Foundry, and multi-agent review for enhanced security.
- An open-core public signal feed (RSS/JSON) with forge-proof ed25519 licensing, offering a free tier for the commons and a pro tier to sustain the project.
- An autonomous build engine capable of discovering opportunities, processing data, and shipping updates without human intervention.
- 100% keyless and free public data sources, including DefiLlama, Hyperliquid, CoinGecko, Snapshot, Deribit, and public RPCs, ensuring reproducibility and eliminating proprietary data moats.
